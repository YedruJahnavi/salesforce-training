# Day 15 - Data Management

## Data Quality Problems

Data quality problems can affect business operations and reporting.

### Common Problems
- Duplicate records
- Missing information
- Incorrect data entry
- Outdated records
- Inconsistent data formats

### Examples
- Duplicate student records
- Invalid email addresses
- Missing contact details

---

## Migration Discussion

Data migration is the process of moving data from one system to another.

### Migration Process
1. Extract data
2. Clean the data
3. Validate records
4. Import into Salesforce
5. Verify imported data

### Challenges
- Duplicate records
- Missing values
- Incorrect mappings
- Data inconsistencies

---

## Duplicate Prevention Ideas

Duplicate records can be prevented using:

- Validation Rules
- Duplicate Rules
- Matching Rules
- Unique Fields
- Regular Data Reviews

### Benefits
- Accurate reports
- Better data quality
- Improved decision-making

---

## Enterprise Risks of Bad Data

Poor-quality data can create serious business problems.

### Risks
- Incorrect reports
- Poor business decisions
- Customer dissatisfaction
- Compliance issues
- Increased operational costs

### Example
Duplicate student records may result in incorrect attendance and registration reports.

---

## Reflection

Data is one of the most important assets in an organization. Maintaining clean and accurate data improves reporting, decision-making, and business efficiency.

### Why is governance important in data management?

Governance ensures that data is accurate, secure, and consistent. It helps organizations follow standards for data entry, validation, access control, and maintenance. Good governance reduces errors and improves data reliability.

Clean data improves accuracy and decision-making.

Because data may contain errors, duplicates, or missing values.

To ensure data accuracy and quality.
