# Day 15 - Data Management

## Data Quality Problems

Data quality problems can affect business operations and reporting.

### Common Problems
- Duplicate records
- Missing information
- Incorrect data entry
- Outdated records
- Inconsistent data formats

### Examples
- Duplicate student records
- Invalid email addresses
- Missing contact details

---

## Migration Discussion

Data migration is the process of moving data from one system to another.

### Migration Steps
1. Extract data from the source system
2. Clean and validate the data
3. Transform data if necessary
4. Import data into Salesforce
5. Verify imported records

### Challenges
- Duplicate records
- Missing values
- Incorrect field mappings
- Data inconsistencies

---

## Duplicate Prevention Ideas

Duplicate records can be prevented using:

- Validation Rules
- Duplicate Rules
- Matching Rules
- Unique Fields
- Regular Data Audits

### Benefits
- Better reporting
- Improved data accuracy
- Reduced confusion
- Better decision-making

---

## Enterprise Risks of Bad Data

Poor-quality data can create serious business problems.

### Risks
- Incorrect reports
- Poor decision-making
- Customer dissatisfaction
- Compliance issues
- Increased operational costs

### Example
If duplicate student records exist, attendance and registration reports may become inaccurate.

---

## Reflection

Data is one of the most valuable assets in an enterprise system. Maintaining clean and accurate data improves reporting, automation, and decision-making. Organizations should use validation, duplicate prevention, and governance practices to ensure high-quality data.

### Why is governance important in data management?

Governance ensures that data is accurate, secure, and consistent. It helps organizations establish rules for data entry, validation, access control, and maintenance. Good governance improves reliability and supports better business decisions.

## Revision Questions

### 1. Why is clean data important?
Clean data improves accuracy and decision-making.

### 2. What problems happen because of duplicate records?
They cause confusion, incorrect reports, and data inconsistencies.

### 3. Why is data migration difficult?
Because data may contain errors, duplicates, or missing values.

### 4. What is Data Loader used for?
To import, export, update, and delete Salesforce records.

### 5. Why should enterprises validate imported data?
To ensure accuracy and prevent bad data from entering the system.

### 6. Why are CSV formats important?
They provide a standard format for data import and export.

### 7. What risks happen during bulk import?
Duplicate records, incorrect mappings, and data loss.

### 8. Why is governance important in data management?
It ensures data quality, security, and compliance.
