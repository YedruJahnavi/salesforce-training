## **CRM Basics – Day 1**

## 1. What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

CRM is a system that helps companies manage customer data, interactions, sales, and support services in one place.

It helps businesses:
- Store customer information
- Track communication
- Improve customer relationships
- Increase sales and productivity

---

## 2. Why Companies Use Salesforce

[Salesforce](https://www.salesforce.com?utm_source=chatgpt.com) is used by companies because it:
- Organizes customer data efficiently
- Improves communication between teams
- Automates business processes
- Tracks sales opportunities
- Provides reports and analytics
- Enhances customer experience

---

## 3. Salesforce Objects

### Account
An Account represents a company or organization.

Examples:
- Amazon
- Infosys
- Apollo Hospital

---

### Contact
A Contact represents a person associated with an Account.

Examples:
- Customer
- Student
- Employee
- Patient

---

### Opportunity
An Opportunity represents a potential business deal or sales process.

Examples:
- Product purchase
- Hospital treatment package
- College admission process

---
